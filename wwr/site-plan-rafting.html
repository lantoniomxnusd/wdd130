Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the site is to inform the public of the recreational services offered, merchandise, booking, FAQ, Reviews to motivate customers to purchase a recreational package, Staff friendliness, safety procedures we implement, contact information for additional help

Audience

The audience is the general public who is interested in enjoying outdoor activities related to rafting

  • Who are the target customers? People who are interested in rafting trips: route, pricing, safety, availability
  • What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered? They will find information that will give them comfort when they see the safety procedures, They will be able to see a map of the rafting route, pricing options, and merchandise after the trip
  • What are their needs that aren't being met? A simple booking system and payment system
  • What packages are available? chill rafting to just enjoy being on the water and have fun, beginner for new rafters, intermediate, andvanced for extreme thrill seekers
