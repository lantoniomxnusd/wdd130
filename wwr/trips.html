South Fork Beginner

This easy, four-mile, class II trip is perfect for families with young children, starting from four years old. It's a quarter-day American River white water rafting adventure, ideal for first-timers seeking a gentle introduction. Bring the little ones along for a memorable and enjoyable experience on the water!

Middle Fork Intermediate

Starting at Oxbow Bend, in a 2,000-foot Sierra canyon, this trip offers exhilarating rafting through world-class rapids to Greenwood Bridge. Covering the entire 18-mile Middle Fork American in a day, we pause midway for a deli-style lunch. Enjoy solo or pair with a South Fork trip for double the fun!

Kaweah Advanced

This 9-mile rafting expedition features non-stop class IV action, including such rapids as Power House, Cyanotic, Holiday Hole, and Suicide Falls. Situated among the dramatic snowcapped mountain peaks of the High Sierra, the headwaters of the Kaweah River plummet to our starting point just miles outside of Sequoia National Park.
